Entrepreneurship Summit 2024

BITS Pilani has a strong reputation for fostering successful startups. The Entrepreneurship Summit aims to unite industry leaders, students, and innovators to explore groundbreaking ideas and startups, featuring pitches and demonstrations of student proof of concepts. We anticipate over 5,000 participants this year, including influential figures from various sectors. The participants will present their innovative ideas and startups, complete with pitches and demonstrations of their proof of concepts.

Date: November 30, 2024

Location: BITS PILANI, PILANI CAMPUS

Schedule

  • 9:00 AM - Keynote Speech
  • 10:00 AM - Artificial Intelligence and Entrepreneurship Panel Discussions
  • 12:00 PM - Lunch Break
  • 1:00 PM - Venture Capital and Funding Panel Discussions
  • 3:00 PM - Building Sustainable Business Models Panel Discussions

Topics

Entrepreneurship, Innovation, Startups
